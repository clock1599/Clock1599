Bridge Around the World

More Stations Coming

The Eastern Railway is surveying the paved railway for locations fit for hosting stations. The ClockEx Rail Co. station is getting upgraded to accommodate both directions of travel, and will receive a fueling port (more on fueling ports below, in News from the South).

Request a station by contacting the commissioners listed in Full Credits.

The Underground Masons

The Eastern Railway began construction of the Underground Masons branch at coordinates 6500, -500.

"The construction won't be all rainbows." — Commissioner Clock1599, after finding the Masons' infrastructure was illegal due to insufficient space between tracks

A Faster Route?

Eastern Railway commissioner Clock1599 has started surveying the area exactly east of The Spawn Bridge, planning ahead to increase capacity and speed.

"Now the track network may look like spaghetti, but The Eastern Railway needs to have capacity and speed." — Clock1599, after approving 2 route plans

Paving the Jungle

The Eastern Railway finished paving the railway from the mangroves at the end of Spawn Bridge to the link between The Eastern Railway and the G.S-W.L.R.
