News from the East

A Faster Route? — Part 2

The Eastern Railway has started work on the route mentioned in last week's newsletter, bringing a total of 6 lanes for use near the spawn area — 2 old mainlines plus a new quad mainline.

A Quick Terraforming Session

A quick terraforming session is scheduled for the "old" mainline, completing its design and bringing it soon into full use.

Expanding the Fleet

New build trains are coming to accommodate The Eastern Railway's new quad-line design, including a new Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) and a new ballaster.
