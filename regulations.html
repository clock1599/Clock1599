Railway Regulations

Regulations will be improved in the following weeks. If you're not sure about a restriction, contact TehGizmoNator or Clock1599.

Gauge

Serverwide tracks should be Standard Gauge, but Wide and Narrow Gauge are still permitted for smaller, coordinated projects.

Have Manners

Please do not steal or leave trains lying around — leaving your train on a track is the railway equivalent of leaving your toys in the middle of the floor. A messy track means no one else can enjoy trains.

If your train blocks a mainline, it will be confiscated and disassembled.
