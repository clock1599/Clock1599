Regulations

Train Sizing

Standard Gauge Trains Should

Floor height
2 blocks above the tracks (same level as the bogey block)
Max height
No taller than 7 blocks including bogey — 2 blocks for bogey and floor, 5 blocks for the interior
Max width
4 blocks or less — a vertical slab, 3 blocks for the interior, and another vertical slab
Max length
No longer than 150 blocks (100 is the sweet spot)
