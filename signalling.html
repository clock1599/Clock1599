Signalling Logic & Priority When Controlling a Train

Follow Standard Signalling and don't abandon trains on tracks. Give way to construction vehicles, and expect delays.

Rules Around Automatic Trains

  • If able to stop without endangering others, give way to automatic trains.
  • Drive with the expanded minimap — it helps you see what's ahead and plan accordingly.

Rules Around Other Conductors

  • While on a mainline, your train has priority.
  • When merging two mainlines on a Y interchange, the outer rails have priority.
